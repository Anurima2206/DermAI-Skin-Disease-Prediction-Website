Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI prediction? +
The AI model is trained on dermatology datasets and provides high accuracy predictions. However, it should be used as a support tool and not a replacement for professional medical advice.
Can this replace a dermatologist? +
No. The platform is designed to assist users with early detection and guidance. Always consult a certified dermatologist for diagnosis and treatment.
Is my uploaded image secure? +
Yes. All images are processed securely and stored only for prediction history purposes.
Is DermAI free? +
Yes. DermAI provides all services for free.
